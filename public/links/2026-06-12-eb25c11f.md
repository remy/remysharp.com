# AI designed pages and prompts

Based on the output of [this article](https://envs.net/~volpe/blog/posts/reduce-slop.html) the author has styled their project with different AI prompts.

I think what I find interesting here is after the jQuery mobile themes and especially Bootstrap you come to just visually adapt the most common style. 

What this author has done is asked for the design systems of existing software to be adopted into the code. 

What frustrates me a little bit is that I actually like the "Original" style, which is what AI is pumping out at the moment, as what I see all the time, which just says that I'm not very original…
